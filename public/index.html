Property Summary

Sometimes, a prestigious address isn’t quite enough…which is why we’re offering some of the finest contemporary homes to be developed in Bolton that give you more than just location, location, location. Although, easy access to the M61, close proximity to Bolton town centre, good local facilities and arguably one of the most superb settings in the area means you don’t have to compromise on anything, and neither have we. Designed to our own exacting standards, and inspired by some of the best ‘superhomes’ in the North West, the properties offer the benefit of all the modern facilities of a newly built home, with a distinct architectural heritage reflecting a design formed in the Arts & Crafts tradition but with a modern finesse to suit luxury 21st Century living.




Location
Set in a stunning mature woodland at the edge of the highly desirable suburban area of Lostock, overlooking countryside to the North and directly adjacent to Bolton Golf Club, Burnthwaite offers a rural retreat within close proximity of urban facilities.

Bolton Town Centre, is within 3 miles via Chorley New Road, the M61 is accessible approximately 2.5 miles via Junction ?? at Middlebrook making the development well placed for commuting to the wider region or within the local area. Train stations are located at Bolton or Lostock, only 1.5m away with a 30 minute journey to Manchester Piccadilly. The highly regarded independent Bolton School is within 2 miles.



THE PROPERTIES





SPECIFICATION
Forshaw are proud of their attention to detail in producing homes of the highest standard. As developers we strive to create homes of outstanding quality. Burnthwaite offers an outstanding level of specification, both in terms of the equipment and design features included as standard, and the infrastructure to supports further upgrades and enhancements. We understand that a purchaser may specific requirements for the technology and audio/visual installations we ensure that all requirements can be accommodated.

Kitchens
We’re able to offer a choice of some of the finest Kitchen manufacturers to form the heart of your new home, whether you want a modern German kitchen or the traditionalism of an English cabinetmaker, we’ll provide a budget figure as standard and allow you to design your dream kitchen either within that budget or at additional cost if necessary.

Bathrooms
We’ve partnered with Villeroy & Boch and provide a selection some of their finest sanitaryware and bathroom furniture from an extensive range of styles with complimentary brassware from Hansgrohe and Vado.

Cable Infrastructure
The property features a comprehensive structured wiring installation to allow for the flexible installation, integration and control of lighting, heating, data, media and security. Its seamless integration into the building allows for continued introduction and integration of new technologies for years to come, without the need for invasive installation works. In addition to the integral cable infrastructure, the following systems will be installed ready for operation on completion:

Lighting
A sophisticated mood lighting system is available as an upgrade to allow preset scenes to be stored and recalled across the entire property. The system will include the internal and external lighting and can provide advanced functionality such as occupancy sensing, automatic day and night modes and a holiday feature.

Windows Treatments
Aluminium double glazed windows are fitted throughout the properties including bi-fold doors where fitted. As an upgrade all living and bedroom spaces can be pre-wired for motorised blinds, which can be operated as part of the lighting control system to be configured to open and close automatically or be controlled manually from keypads.

Heating
Separately zoned, water based under-floor heating system to the whole property, with discrete temperature sensors in each room. In addition to standard time-clock features, the system also features advanced controls included frost protection and holiday modes.

Data
A wireless and wired gigabit ethernet network will provide wireless coverage throughout the house, with wired data sockets in key rooms. This will allow the connection of any wired or wireless network device and with your choice of Internet Service Provider (ISP), allow access to a broadband service.

Bespoke Audio & Media Package (available as an upgrade)
To suit your individual requirements a Multi-room audio distribution system can be installed which provides the ability to listen to music from a selection of sources in either a single room, or across a number of rooms simultaneously.

Video
Each home will be pre-wired for Freeview, FreeSat and Sky in every key room. Video images from the CCTV system can also be distributed to every TV outlet as part of the security package. The cable infrastructure also supports the installation of a system which can distribute Full HD and 3D images from Sky, Blu-Ray or media servers to every display location.

Cinema
Media rooms will feature a fully integrated 7.1 cinema installation with low-level lighting. The purchaser can choose to upgrade this system with a multitude of custom options, including projection, tiered and motorised seating and 3D systems.

Control
An integrated control system will provide seamless and intuitive operation of the systems mentioned, providing a single interface for controlling the house. This system can be upgraded almost limitlessly, but could include custom interface designs and complete remote access.

Security
CCTV access systems will be installed as standard, providing entry control to the development from the secure main gate. A fully-featured intruder alarm will be installed and we also offer the ability to upgrade to a fully bespoke security solution in partnership with our specialist system providers.

TECHNICAL SPECIFICATION
As with all Forshaw properties, energy efficiency and environmental considerations have been a key part of the design philosophy for Burnthwaite, and the following features are included:

Water Collection and Recycling
A rainwater collection system, fed from the roof drains and guttering, stores water in a local tank, which then supplies the garden irrigation and toilet flush systems.

Hot Water System
A state of the art solar tube system, installed in conjunction with high efficiency condensing boilers, together reduce the fossil fuel fed requirements by 50-75%. This system is further enhanced through the use of under-floor heating throughout the property, which is one of the most efficient methods of dispensing heat available.

Lighting System
In addition to the aesthetic and practical advantages that the mood lighting system offers, it is additionally programmed to ‘trim’ the lighting levels to a level which is imperceptible to the human eye, but has the result of dramatically reducing electrical consumption and increasing lamp life. Occupancy sensing in utility areas also ensures that lights are not left on unnecessarily.

Household Waste Recycling
The property features a dedicated recycling area, to allow for the uncluttered storage of card, paper, bottles, tins and plastics. This area has also been designed with capacity for a waste compactor, which can reduce the volume of household waste to approximately one third of its natural volume.

Appliances
All the installed and integrated appliances will feature an energy efficiency rating of ‘A’ or higher.

Development

THE DEVELOPMENT

Set in 15 acres, comprising the grounds of the former Burnthwaite Hall, the development will provide 5 new detached dwellings. Each home is be provided with its own individual character, through design and situation but all share a common theme; to provide the best in contemporary house development with character and distinction.

The development will provide a real ‘community’ with family lifestyles high on the list of our objectives. Therefore safety and security are a key consideration which is achieved by providing a secure gated development, with access controlled by residents directly from their homes.

Quality of environment is also a priority, therefore we’ve incorporated as much of the natural features of the site as possible and even added some more. We like trees, we like hedgerows, we like landscaped features. We don’t like bland, ill-conceived streetscenes and we’re pretty sure most home buyers don’t either.

The development has been designed to blend with the adjacent environment, utilising the woodland setting to its best advantage and creating a harmony with the natural surrounding, we recognise the importance of creating a home that respects its surroundings rather than imposing on it. How many of us recall the time spent as children playing out in the open countryside, exploring forests and copses? Sadly, modern lifestyles mean many of our children aren’t exposed to the natural environment, at Burnthwaite it’s at the bottom of your garden as the whole 15 acres has been divided between each of the 5 homes to offer each substantial grounds with differing character.

We’ve also taken care of the responsibilities that a biologically diverse environment can require. The woodland within the site is to be maintained under a management agreement, further details of which are available on request. Therefore residents can have all of the enjoyment of a quality setting without the headache and responsibility of maintenance or management of these important aspects of the development.

PURCHASE OPTIONS

We are pleased to be able to offer a number of options for the purchase of your new home at Burnthwaite:

Traditional Purchase

Your new home can be secured by payment of a reservation fee, following which we will work with legal advisers in progressing to an exchange of contracts. We will keep you updated throughout construction of your new home and when appropriate agree a completion date at which point the full balance of the purchase price is payable. Stamp Duty would also be payable on the full purchase price at the appropriate rate.

Plot Purchase, Design and Build

This offers an innovative and cost-efficient method of purchasing your property. A reservation fee is paid for your selected plot, following which we will exchange contracts and complete the purchase of the land element of your property. Simultaneously we will enter into a design and build contract under which staged payments are made until completion of your home. This offers cost savings against stamp duty as only the land purchase element attracts the duty charge.
